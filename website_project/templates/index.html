We work together as a team.

Our organization is constantly working together on new projects for the members to learn new skills and make friends along the way. Each member brings their own set of skills and experiences.

The East Stroudsburg University Computer Science Organization offers many opportunities for ESU Computer Science and Computer Security students. You do not need to be in one of the majors to be a part of this club. We offer many practical opportunities for students to apply their CPSC knowledge. We are always looking for new members to join and are very friendly and welcoming.

We offer things like:

  • Experience building
  • Networking with your peers
  • Project opportunities
  • Community service events / opportunities
  • Virtual conferences
  • Assistance with Computer Science / Security concepts
